Greninja

Greninja is a Water/Dark type Pokemon, known as the Ninja Pokemon. It is the final evolution of Froakie and has a unique ability called Battle Bond, which transforms it into Ash-Greninja.

Greninja has strong attacks such as Water Shuriken and Night Slash, and is a popular choice among competitive players in Pokemon battles.

Here are some more facts about Greninja:

  • Greninja was first introduced in Generation VI of the Pokemon games.
  • Greninja's design was inspired by frogs and ninjas.
  • Greninja's special ability, Battle Bond, is triggered when it defeats an opponent in battle.
